Not convinced yet? Let’s look at SousChef’s unique features.

Customise your cuts

You can select the number of pieces you want, or the thickness of each slice.

Voice control

Busy preparing other parts of your meal? Or maybe a disabled person in your family needs help? Just tell your SousChef what to cut.

Your safety is our priority

SousChef will include a protective lid that will be closed while it’s cutting your food. If you accidentally open it, SousChef will immediately stop cutting.

While not cutting, the blade will retract to a safe position to allow you to take out your food.
